🏛 Our Story

About Greenfield 📄 Static

🏫

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1975 by Mr. Rajendra Prasad Sharma, Greenfield Public School began with 80 students and a vision to bring quality education to Hyderabad. Today we stand as one of Telangana's premier CBSE institutions.

We serve over 2,400 students from Nursery to Class XII, backed by 120+ experienced educators across 8 acres of modern infrastructure.

Our alumni are doctors, engineers, artists, entrepreneurs and civil servants, carrying Greenfield's values across the world.

🎯

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that empowers every student with knowledge, values and skills to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

🌱

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where academic rigor meets holistic development — nurturing confident, ethical citizens.

👨‍🏫

Dr. Suresh Kumar Rao

Principal, Greenfield Public School
"At Greenfield, we believe every child carries the seeds of greatness. Our role is to provide the right environment to help those seeds bloom. I warmly welcome you to the Greenfield family."
